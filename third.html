Help Us Fight!

"Why is block so special," you ask? Blocking schedule is crucial to the betterment of our student's academic prowess because it allows the student time to confide in their teacher about their problems with homework, allows them to better manage their time, and rewards them with a much needed break when they have finished their work. However, now that block is gone, students stay up innumerous hours in order to finish the enormus pile of homework that is due the next day. This in turn hinders thier ability to focus and stunts their academic progress.

You can help us fight to bring back block through:

  • Contacting Mrs. Bruce, the CVHS Principal, at (909) 799-2300
  • Advocating for the block schedule to your local school board
  • Signing Our Petition
